# Page2Email
A Chrome extension that captures the current page as a screenshot or PDF and lets you email it instantly (via mailto or your webmail like Outlook/Gmail) to save receipts, confirmations, and completed forms.

# Never lose the “confirmation page” again

When you finish a payment, submit an application, or complete an online form, the most important details are often stuck on a page you can’t easily save—no download button, no clean print view, and copying/pasting is error-prone.

This extension lets you **capture the current page as a screenshot or PDF** and **email it immediately** so you can keep a reliable record.

---

## What it does

- **One-click capture** of the active tab as:
  - **Screenshot (PNG/JPG)** for pixel-perfect proof
  - **PDF** for easy archiving and sharing
- **Send by email in seconds**
  - Open your default email app using **mailto:** with the subject/body pre-filled, or
  - Use **Gmail / Outlook on the web** when you’re already signed in (where supported) for a smoother “send now” flow
- **Built for “proof & records” workflows**
  - Payment/checkout confirmations
  - Appointment/booking pages
  - Submitted forms and application receipts
  - Order summaries, tracking pages, support case confirmations

---

## Why it’s useful

- **Faster than copy/paste:** share the whole page with context, not fragments.
- **Better documentation:** keep an immutable snapshot of what was shown at the moment you completed a step.
- **Easy compliance & teamwork:** send to yourself, accounting, support, or a shared mailbox.
